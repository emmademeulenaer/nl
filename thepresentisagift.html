THE PRESENT IS A GIFT

This assignment consisted of one sentence: “The present is a gift”. I tried to focus on 2 things: the materials on one hand and working spontaneously on the other, something I find difficult to do (I overthink everything). That is how I came to this work: every day I made a spontaneous composition of fabrics. Searching for an interesting mix of colours, forms and textures I began to see these as little diary fragments. Certain elements connect in such way they make each other stronger, layering becomes the main focus and contrasts are being challenged. This project was for me a way to adjust to a new system and changing my work process. Starting from a concept, finding the right technique and finishing with a good image: that was the goal.
