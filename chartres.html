OLVOO

For this project we had to choose a historical theme. I based my designs on the stained glass windows of the gothic kathedral in Chartres. The main focus was the contrast between the gleaming colours of the glass and the harsh grid in-between them. This was a year of practicing techniques, I learned about industrial knitting, how to improve my weaving skills, I had screen printing workshops and practiced on the embroidery-machine.
