MOUWPIES

This year, I can choose freely what I want to do, as there is no assignment. We started out writing down where our previous work was centred around. For me, layering of textures was something that always came back. I also have a great interest towards the body, and how textiles work around it. I’m doing research on clothing, how a piece of clothing acts underneath another layer of clothing, and another one. The piece of clothing interests me as an object. How the fabric is three-dimensional around the body and how it is two-dimensional on its own. The pattern is for me a symbol of this system. It is just a flat collection of pieces of paper, but at the same time it acts as the source of the garment. I’m using the monotyping technique to flatten the layers and enhance the structures of the fabric. By folding the fabric and printing it like that, an illusion of depth is created within the prints.
